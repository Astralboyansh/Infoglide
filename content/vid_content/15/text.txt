TDP leader alleges 'conspiracy to kill' Chandrababu Naidu in jail
VIJAYAWADA: Telugu Desam Party (TDP) national general secretary Nara Lokesh on Thursday alleged that the Yuvajana Sramika Rythu Congress Party (YSRCP) government is "trying to kill" his father and party chief N Chandrababu Naidu in Rajahmundry central jail in Andhra Pradesh\u2019s East Godavari district.
It appears that they have planned to "eliminate Naidu" in the jail by arresting him in a false case without any evidence and by ensuring that he does not get bail, Lokesh alleges.

Lokesh took to microblogging website X on Thursday suspecting that a "conspiracy is being hatched" to harm the opposition leader in jail.
Mentioning the recent death of a remand prisoner \u2014 Ganjeti Veera Venkata Satyanarayana who reportedly died after contracting dengue fever inside the jail \u2014 Lokesh expressed concern over the safety of Naidu inside the prison as there are too many mosquitos.
He suspected that a plan was being implemented by exposing Naidu to mosquitos so that he would also get vector-borne disease like dengue. The TDP leader said that despite alerting the jail authorities about mosquito menace inside the jail, no action was taken by them.
The opposition leader, who is under Z+ category security, was lodged in the jail by implicating him in a false case, he said. Lokesh further said that the chief minister Y S Jagan Mohan Reddy would be held responsible if anything happened to Naidu inside the jail.
